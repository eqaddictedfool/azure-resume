    Rollin

    2018

    During a "Hack-A-Thon" styled event sponsored at Bungie. I lead a small team of IT staff to redesign end user workstations. This project was inspired by the cost in time and money we would spend paying external companies to come in and move dev equipment each quarter. We would reduce this by simplifying the workstation so that each user could disconnect a single ethernet cable and a single power cable from a floor box and roll the desk to where they were needed making the team much more agile. This design would later be adopted during a studio redesign as the default workstation standard.
